Welcome to Daily Disc, our first ever game, made for iOS with Godot.

Features

Simply drag back and launch the disc toward the goals, being careful to avoid obstacles. You can also curve your shots and do a mid-air double jump for extra precision.

There are infinite levels, since maps are randomly generated, but each day you get to make an attempt at the Daily Level. Compare your score with your friends to see who did best!

We hope to add in some more maps soon, but in the meantime you can collect multiple disc skins, and unlock a handful of achievements!

Feedback
